Frequently Asked Questions

Is this pricing set in stone?

No. The above pricing charts are meant to give you rates of our average programs. We'll first want to get a better understanding of your campaign goals and then we can give you an estimate specific to your campaign.

How will I be billed?

We invoice most clients monthly and they pay by check. If you want to pay by recurring credit card or PayPal, we can accommodate that was well.

How long is the typical campaign?

The lion's share of the campaigns we have worked on have lasted approximately 3 months. But again, this will vary program to program. Some campaigns need to be quick hitters, others are about creating longer lasting relationships with influencers and bloggers.

Can I talk to someone directly about program pricing?

Certainly. Send an email to newbusiness@next-impulse.com and we'll answer any questions you have. Alternatively, you can call us at 650-427-0021.
